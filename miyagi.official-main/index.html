Our Vision

Miyagi X Pulse envisions a world where blockchain operates with the stability of natural energy systems. Inspired by bio-synchronization and quantum signal theory, the network is engineered to create transparent, fair, and self-adjusting digital ecosystems at scale.

The Quantum Glow White Paper outlines our commitment to long-term sustainability, minimal hardware dependency, and enterprise-level resilience.

Prime Mobile Pulse Node Technology

The Prime Mobile Pulse Node (PMPN) framework enables any modern smartphone to participate in network validation through the Proof of Pulse & Participation (PoPP) model.

This significantly reduces operational cost while increasing scalability, enabling blockchain-grade security through distributed mobile infrastructure without relying on traditional mining hardware.

Current Phase — Genesis Test Network
