Tokenomics Overview

Miyagi X Pulse introduces a sustainable mobile-node earning ecosystem where users generate rewards through Proof-of-Pulse (PoPP) mining, boosts, and long-term participation. Our model ensures fair distribution and ecosystem longevity.

Mining & Node Reward System

Base Mining Rate

Every user earns MIX by running the mobile node. Rewards depend strictly on uptime, device activity, and connection consistency.

6-Hour Boost

Users can activate a 6-hour boost to temporarily increase mining output, encouraging daily engagement and network validation.

Referral Bonus

Inviting users generates a percentage bonus based on referral activity. Strict anti-abuse measures ensure network fairness.

Long-Term Multiplier

Users who remain active over time receive a higher earning multiplier, rewarding commitment over short-term farming.

Vesting & Utility

Vesting Schedule

  • Team: 12-month lock, then 48-month linear vesting.
  • Marketing: 6-month lock, 12-month vesting.
  • Ecosystem: Released based on milestone achievements.

MIX Token Utility

  • In-App: Mining speed upgrades & boost activation.
  • Governance: Vote on updates and parameters.
  • Staking: Passive rewards & advanced node tiers.

Sustainability Model

Miyagi X Pulse follows a fair, transparent structure. The majority of tokens are allocated to the community through mining. Anti-inflationary measures include a fixed maximum supply, scheduled mining reductions (halvings), and token burn mechanisms tied to premium feature usage.
