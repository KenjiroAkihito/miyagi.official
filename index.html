Prime Mobile Pulse Node Technology

The Prime Mobile Pulse Node (PMPN) is our signature innovation. Each mobile device acts as a mini-node, capable of validating micro-transactions through energy-efficient Proof of Pulse & Participation (PoPP). No expensive hardware — just smart connectivity that rewards engagement and uptime.

By distributing node operations across smartphones, Miyagi X Pulse achieves unprecedented scalability while maintaining quantum-resistant encryption integrity.
